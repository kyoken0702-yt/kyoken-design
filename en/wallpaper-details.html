Fire-retardant Seamless Wallpaper
Ultimate beauty without joints.

For hotel suites and luxury stores. Unlike general wallpaper, our "seamless" specification uses a single piece of fabric for heights up to 3 meters. Because there are no joints, it prevents peeling due to aging and achieves overwhelming design quality.
