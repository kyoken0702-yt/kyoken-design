Measurement Guide / How to Measure

01

Width (W) Measurement

Measure the distance between the fixed runners (static hooks) at both ends of the rail.
※Order Size = Measured Size × 1.05 (Allowance)

02

Height (H) Measurement

● Patio Window
Measure from hook ring to floor, then subtract 1cm.

● Waist-high Window
Measure from hook ring to window frame bottom, then add 15cm.

03

Hook Type Choice (A or B)

A
Hook

Exposes rail. Ideal for ceiling rails, decorative rods, or smooth sliding.

B
Hook

Covers rail. Ideal for wall rails to minimize light leakage from the top.
